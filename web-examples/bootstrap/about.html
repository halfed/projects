Who We Are

  • William J. Sleasman, PC
  • William J. Sleasman
  • Janice L. Sleasman

William J. Sleasman, PC is a forward-thinking Certified Public Accounting Firm that provides a wide array of services and has strong collaborative working relationships with other accounting firms. The firm includes William J. Sleasman, Certified Public Accountant; Allen Tanner; and Janice Sleasman. We provide services to a diversified clientele.

Our mission for the past 30 years has been to provide our clients with "Beyond Routine Services." We serve and fill those needs with accurate and timely service. We go one step beyond providing them with more than many other firms provide. Our clients count on us and we are there for them.

We believe that clients need non-traditional services in addition to traditional services, to succeed and excel. Non-traditional services include meeting with clients to clearly define their goals and helping them to achieve them We do this by using alternate means of providing financial information and staying in touch with our clients when they need us.

Please contact us if you would like to discuss what our team can do for you.

Bill graduated from the University of Oregon with a Bachelor of Science degree. At the time, Bill had the distinction of being the President of the honorary accounting fraternity, Beta Alpha Psi. After receiving a number of offers from national CPA firms, he accepted a position with Price Waterhouse & Co.

After spending several years with Price Waterhouse, he accepted a position with a local CPA firm where he provided services to family-owned businesses. After that Bill started his own firm.

In 1980, Bill incorporated his firm and moved it to Clackamas, Oregon. As his practice grew, Bill became active in a number of civic and Christian organizations including the Clackamas Rotary Club, Christian Chamber of Commerce of the Northwest and the North Clackamas School District Board.

Bill believes that lasting relationships is the real bottom line. He enjoys spending time with his family, golfing, gardening, hiking, and canoeing.

Janice L. Sleasman graduated from Warner Pacific College with a Bachelor of Science Degree, majoring in Human Development with a minor in Music. She held a number of positions during her career. Janice was a legal secretary with law firms in Seattle, Washington as well as Corvallis and Portland, Oregon. She owned her own typing business with the majority of the service transcribing for court reporters. She held the position of administrative assistant at both large and small companies. She is a QuickBooks ProAdvisor. Janice loves to travel, hike, canoe, spend as much time as possible with family including grandkids, playing piano and organ, plus going to concerts.

Allen R. Tanner represents the future of the firm. He joined the firm in 2008. He graduated from Portland State University with a Bachelors of Accounting degree. Allen is a QuickBooks Pro-Advisor and works with clients resolving their QuickBooks questions. He provides the firm with financial reporting and income tax compliance services. He is studying to take the CPA exam. Allen has developed valuable working relationships with a number of clients.

Allen is a lifetime resident of Clackamas County, Oregon, so he is very familiar with the Portland Metropolitan area. In 2012, Allen married another Oregon native, Kira, who is a Registered Nurse working with children at a pediatric clinic. When away from work they are both very involved in a number of activities with their church and community. Allen enjoys helping underprivileged youth, hunting, camping and spending time with his family.
